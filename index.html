what is this?

Data, Set, Go! is a hackathon that not only challenge the contestants to solve a real problem with different Data Science techniques, but also offers training and networking sessions.

This is challenge was created for students who are into Data Science & Machine Learning and want to test their knowledge and learn more about this area, either through workshop sessions or through more informal contact with companies.

when & where?

This hackathon is happening on 12th, 13th and 14th of February and it takes place at Department of Computer Engineering, University of Coimbra.

A place that has ideal conditions for the dynamization of such a diverse event organized through various rooms, auditoriums and social spaces, to work and socialize throughout the event.

who is organizing?

jeKnowledge and JEST are collaborating for the first time on a project to develop an innovative and technological event on a modern and high impact theme. An event not to be missed!

jeKnowledge

jeKnowledge is a FCTUC Junior Enterprise. We are 11 years old and we are a multidisciplinary team that wants the world and we are known to be different. Technology and Innovation is our area.

JEST

JEST is a Junior Initiative focused on Data Science counting on the dynamism of its elements coming from 7 distinct FCTUC courses! Providing data-based solutions for life sciences.
